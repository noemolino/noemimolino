About Me

I’m a Front-End Developer with a strong foundation in digital marketing, which allows me to bridge the gap between user-centric design and technical implementation. My hybrid background helps me approach development not only from a code perspective, but also with a strategic mindset focused on communication, engagement, and user behavior.

My work is centered around building modular and scalable UI systems, writing clean, semantic, and maintainable code using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and ensuring seamless integration with content management platforms such as Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) and Contentstack. I pay close attention to detail when implementing interfaces that are both visually refined and performance-optimized.

I thrive in collaborative environments, especially in cross-functional teams, where I can contribute ideas, share knowledge, and help turn design concepts into interactive, user-friendly products. I enjoy tackling complex problems, improving development workflows, and aligning technical solutions with business goals.

At the moment, I’m actively focusing on deepening my understanding of modern JavaScript, refining my CSS architecture skills, and advancing in web accessibility to ensure the experiences I build are usable and inclusive for all users. I’m always curious, eager to learn, and committed to delivering high-quality front-end experiences that make a real difference.
